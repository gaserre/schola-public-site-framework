Gilbert & Sullivan Links

The Web's major Gilbert & Sullivan resource is the Gilbert and Sullivan Archive Site.

Over 600 people around the world read and contribute to SavoyNet, the G&S mailing list.

In the San Francisco Bay Area, the other major performing groups are the Lamplighters Musical Theatre in San Francisco, and Lyric Theatre, the performing arm of the Gilbert and Sullivan Society of San Jose. For the most up-to-date worldwide list of G&S performing groups, visit the list at the Gilbert and Sullivan Archive.
