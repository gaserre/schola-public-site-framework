How to get in touch with the Stanford Savoyards

Our mailing address is

The Stanford Savoyards
ASSU-Tresidder Memorial Union
Stanford, CA 94305

Our production staff for the current season is listed on on our main web site; contact a producer if you're interested in participating in the cast, crew, orchestra, or staff of our shows.

Our mailing list coordinator is Neil Midkiff, neil.midkiff@stanfordalumni.org, phone 408/985-2143.

For tickets, contact the Stanford Ticket Office or see the online ticket links on our main site.

To be added to our postal mailing list, click here, write us at the address above, or sign up in the lobby at a performance. Be sure to specify your complete mailing address including full ZIP code, and tell us whether you want to receive audition announcements (for performers and crew) or ticket order forms (for audience members) or both.

To be added to our electronic mail list, click here.
